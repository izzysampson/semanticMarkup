Brazil: Tem Que Visitar (You Have to Visit)

What is the hype all about?

Brazil has many things that lead to the overall hype. This web page will walk you through a few of the greatest things about Brazil and will also introduce you to my personal experience with this beautiful country.

Think about this: Have you ever seen the movie Rio from Disney? Why is it so good? I would say it's a great movie because it introduces you to the great culture.

What should I do if I go to Brazil?

Brazil is FULL of incredible places to visit. The following places are the only locations I've been to so they're really the only one's I can suggest. Most of these places are in the south-eastern corner of the country.

Here are good states to visit and my personal definition of them:

State of São Paulo
In the state of São Paulo, this is where you find the country's capital. It's a very well-developed city that provides many opportunites for great shopping and city-life activities.
State of Minas Gerais
Minas is the home of the best Brazilian food, pão de quejo (aka cheesy bread). Go to Minas just for the food though because there isn't a lot to do there other than eat.
State of Rio de Janeiro
Rio is the state you go to if you wanna party (it's a little worldy there). But it's absolutley beautiful and is one of the best ways to experience the culture.

Why do I like Brazil?

One of the main reasons I personally like Brazil is because I lived there for 10 months and fell in love with the culture. Here are a few specific reasons:

  1. The people are fun
  2. The weather is good
  3. You make really good memories there. Some of my favorite memories are:
    • Picking food from the trees on the street (snacks to go)
    • Sporatic dance parties whenever music comes on
    • Becoming a part of a social unit, one that is super accepting

One downfall is that you may get cat-called walking on the street if you're an American.

Just go to Brazil, it'll be worth it.
